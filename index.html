Indian Manufacturing Summit

With the exponentially evolving landscape of Manufacturing from a physical factory to a smart factory, it has been the engine of every successful economy across the globe. With Government of India's initiatives like "Make in India" and the current corporate tax reduction, India is all set to achieve manufacturing supremacy.

The 8th Edition India Manufacturing Summit is a catalyst mobilizing the expertise of industry thought leaders and radical efficiency improvement opportunities presented by competitive solution providers under one platform.

The inevitable need for adopting Smart Manufacturing leads to the main focus of our agenda this year. The industry experts will discuss about Leadership & Manufacturing Innovation Strategies, Safety and Sustainable Manufacturing and disruptive technologies such as Advanced Robotics in R&D Labs, Computer Vision in Warehouses, IIoT, AI and more, with the aim of shaping the industry.

Trends in Manufacturing Sector

Leadership & Talent management

India needs to significantly enhance its manufacturing sector’s competitiveness to create the large number of jobs needed to reap the benefits of our demographic dividend, leverage opportunities in the global trade and reduce imports in key areas.

Smart Manufacturing 4.0

Companies are creating digitally enhanced product lines and services to improve operational efficiency. This revolution is hence moving manufacturers from mass production to mass customization, through a digital supply network.

Disruptive Technologies

Artificial Intelligence, IIOT, Additive Manufacturing etc. are emerging as an efficient way to help manufacturing support their Digital Transformation Journey.

Lights Out Manufacturing

It doesn’t rely on continual human presence. It is a paradigm shift to pre-programmed robots performing fully automated tasks.

Digital Twins

A digital replica that utilizes technologies such as virtual reality by making use of data & graphics modeling for perfectly creating a virtual model of any equipment.

Quality Management

Quality policy is more like a philosophy for any organization. Quality planning is integrated with the vision, mission and organizational strategy for every manufacturing industry where process efficiency, quality metrics, reduced waste etc. have started attracting the manufacturers.
