Why Attend Indian Manufacturing Summit ?

With a one-day initiative designed to bring together 120+ manufacturing heads to discuss critical challenges affecting the manufacturing sector in a fading economy, India Manufacturing Summit is going to be the platform where we discuss evolving landscapes of the manufacturing sector and turn insights into actions.

It is a dynamic event packed with thought leadership sessions and executive vision panels, one-on-one executive exchanges through speed networking, one-to-one meetings and extended networking opportunities.
